# How to Install Access to Jobs

A step-by-step guide to download and start using the Access to Jobs skill in your Claude account.

**Time required:** Under 2 minutes. No technical skills needed.

---

## Step 1 — Download the Skill File

Click the link below to download the skill file to your computer:

**[Download access-to-jobs.skill](https://github.com/dougdevitre/access-to-jobs/releases/latest/download/access-to-jobs.skill)**

The file will download to your Downloads folder. It is a small file (under 200 KB) and safe to open.

> **Note:** If your browser asks what to do with the file, choose **Save File**. Do not try to open it directly — you will upload it to Claude in the next step.

---

## Step 2 — Sign In to Claude

1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) in your web browser
2. Sign in with your account (or create a free account if you don't have one)

---

## Step 3 — Open Settings

1. Look for your **profile icon** in the bottom-left corner of the screen
2. Click it, then click **Settings**

---

## Step 4 — Upload the Skill

1. In Settings, click the **Skills** tab
2. Click **Add skill**
3. Select the `access-to-jobs.skill` file you downloaded in Step 1
4. Wait a moment for the upload to complete — you should see "Access to Jobs" appear in your skills list

---

## Step 5 — Start Using It

Go back to the chat and try any of these:

| Say this | What happens |
|----------|-------------|
| "I need help finding a job" | The skill walks you through your options |
| "Write me a resume" | Creates a tailored, professional resume |
| "Apply for this job" + paste a job posting | Generates a resume, cover letter, and application email |
| "I have an interview tomorrow" | Gives you 5 practice questions with sample answers |
| "What programs am I eligible for?" | Screens you for 20+ workforce programs |
| "I just got hired" | Creates a 30/60/90-day success plan |

**Tip:** For the best results, paste the full job posting into the chat along with your request.

---

## Updating to a New Version

When a new version is released:

1. Download the latest `.skill` file using the same link above
2. In Claude Settings → Skills, remove the old version
3. Upload the new file

You can check for new versions on the [Releases page](https://github.com/dougdevitre/access-to-jobs/releases).

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see a Skills tab in Settings"

Skills may require a Claude Pro, Team, or Enterprise account. Check [claude.ai/pricing](https://claude.ai) for current plan details.

### "The skill doesn't seem to activate"

Try being specific about employment. Instead of "help me," say "help me find a job" or "write me a resume." The skill activates on employment-related requests.

### "I want to use a version customized for my state"

The default skill includes Missouri as the reference state. If your state has a custom version available, check the [states/ directory](https://github.com/dougdevitre/access-to-jobs/tree/main/states) for state-specific implementations, or contact your local workforce agency to see if they offer a customized version.

### "Can I use this on mobile?"

Yes. The Claude mobile app supports skills. Follow the same steps — download the file, then upload it through the app's Settings.
