Key Features

🇺🇸

Government Applications

Federal resume (4-6 pages), KSA/CCAR narratives, civil service exam prep, veteran preference, Schedule A.

💰

Total Compensation Calculator

Compare up to 3 offers: salary + health insurance + retirement + PTO + all benefits. Federal vs. private sector comparison.

📊

HR Performance Dashboard

75+ KPIs across recruitment, retention, performance, compensation, compliance, workforce planning, and engagement.

💻

Workday Integration

Copy/paste content for job requisitions, interview feedback, performance reviews, compensation changes, and onboarding.

🌎

State-Deployable

Replace 4 files to deploy to any U.S. state. Missouri reference implementation included with all 114 counties.

🤝

Population-Aware

Auto-detects and adjusts for 15 barrier populations: veterans, justice-involved, youth, disability, and more.
